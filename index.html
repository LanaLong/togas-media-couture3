For almost a century, Togas has been a pioneer in quality textiles, bringing luxury and impeccable style to homes and venues all around the world. While Togas remains proud of its history and Greek heritage, it is a global manufacturer and retailer in its own field, having earned the confidence of its worldwide customers by creating over 3,000 products that excel in quality, beauty and style.

Providing exceptional bespoke customer service with made-to-measure collections for you, our customers, is our top priority. We develop unique products and apply them to every need, therefore enabling you to find the perfect design and fabric in order to master your home decoration. Each of our bespoke items is an exceptionally balanced and harmonized masterpiece. The meticulous attention to detail and the professional craftsmanship used are essential to our service.

Our custom-made philosophy is based on family values. Utilizing the invaluable knowledge that has been passed down from one generation to the next, our team is a group of highly experienced professionals who can lead you to the best home solutions. Our in-house design team determines a creative and unique style, while utilizing Togas’ century-old weaving techniques in order to create exclusive, high-quality fabrics. The fabrics we use are exercised throughout the process of production and creation of each of our products. Silk, cotton, stonewashed organic linens and bamboo are but a few of the extensive fabrics that our customers can choose from.

We incorporate classic motifs into modern designs, reinventing vintage looks to suit the contemporary customer; apart from the popular hand-drawn floral, classic and geometric designs, the Art-Deco motif is very typical. Our products are inspired by our close involvement with different countries and our interest in their cultural trends. Every pattern is infused with feelings and images of nature, art and culture. When it comes to made-to-measure bespoke tailoring, Togas has the team and the expertise to help you realize your vision of a beautiful interior. From the upscale eclectic to the urban chic, Togas has something for everybody.

Drapery hardware can be an integral part of your window treatment and another decorative element with which you can create your final look. Curtain rods, poles, finials, brackets and rings are available in both modern and traditional styles in order to complement your decor. Our story

After the end of WW1, the young and talented Ilias Togas moves to Athens from Arcadia, a region in southern Greece. Ilias founds a small atelier at the dawn of Art Deco epoque. The high-quality craftsmanship makes Ilias Togas well-known throughout Greece.

WW2 comes to its end and Ilias Togas marries Katerina Schik, the daughter of a famous textile-manufacturing dynasty of Austrian origin. The love match leads to the union of the two businesses. Togas now combines production with retail and a unique family business empire is established.

In the early 1970s sons of Ilias, Pavlos and Dimitris Togas, inherit the family business. The brothers decide to expand the company into the home textile industry. They use the recourses of the factory and the embroidery machines while utilizing the expertise handed down to them by their father.

Since 1995, the family story grows even more with the arrival of Pavlos’ son, Kostas Togas. He is the one that will initiate the expansion of the Togas sales network to new markets all around the world. Custom service is now Togas’ priority, mastering the service and bringing it to another level.
