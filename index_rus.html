EXCELLENCE IN CUSTOM SERVICE  

For almost a century, Togas has been a pioneer in quality textiles, bringing luxury and impeccable style to homes and venues all around the world. While the brand remains proud of its Greek heritage, it has a global presence, having earned the confidence of its worldwide customers by creating over 3,000 products that excel in quality and beauty.

Providing exceptional bespoke customer service for you, our customers, is our top priority. We develop unique products and apply them to every need, therefore enabling you to find the perfect design and fabric in order to master your home decoration. Each of our bespoke items is an exceptionally balanced and harmonized masterpiece. The meticulous attention to detail and the professional craftsmanship used are essential to our brand and our service.

Our custom-made philosophy is based on family values. Utilizing the invaluable knowledge that has been passed down from one generation to the next as well as Togas’ century-old weaving techniques, our team can lead you to the best home solutions.

The fabrics we use are exercised throughout the process of production and creation of our products. Silk, cotton, stonewashed organic linens and bamboo are but a few of the extensive fabrics that our customers can choose from. We incorporate classic motifs into modern designs, reinventing vintage looks to suit the contemporary customer; apart from the popular hand-drawn floral, classic and geometric designs, the Art-Deco motif is very typical. Our products are inspired by our close involvement with different countries and our interest in their cultural trends. Every pattern is infused with feelings of nature, art and culture.

When it comes to made-to-measure bespoke tailoring, Togas has the team and the expertise to help you realize the vision of a beautiful interior. From the upscale eclectic to the urban chic, Togas has something for everybody.

Upholstery

A unique range of the finest
upholstery that creates
a feeling of luxury & style.

Upholstery is a reflection of who we are, an extension of our individuality and style; and Togas is capturing this philosophy through a luxurious custom collection, crafted by the best artisans of our time. Togas has compiled unique furniture to suit all needs and tastes; a range of premium quality that has a strong place in your modern, classic or sophisticated ambiance. Our premium custom furniture is created and produced by leading European factories renown in the world of textiles with the use of traditional techniques, passed from generation to generation, as well as with the use of innovative think “outside the box” high-technology methods. Our custom upholstery is exquisite, yet the value gets stronger with their superior design; our designs are hand-created by our dedicated in-house team, with artistic influences from different cultures, including Chinoiserie, Arabesque and European styles added with classic, floral and geometric motifs and of course the Art Deco style which has become an iconic part of the company’s heritage.

headboards

Hardware

Complete your final
look with the top
hardware items.

Drapery hardware can be an integral part of your window treatment and another decorative element with which you can create your final look. Curtain rods, poles, finials, brackets and rings are available in both modern and traditional styles in order to complement your decor. Rods are made of plastic or metal, they come in different finishes including brass and brushed metal as well as in different shapes such as fluted, twisted etc. Poles are usually made of wood, bamboo or plastic, but are not adjustable. Brackets are the supportive holders that keep rods and poles in place; they can mount onto walls, window frames or even ceilings. Finials are attractive and can keep curtain rings from sliding off the rod or pole while rings can complement your other hardware elements. Our custom options are made in order to fit your lifestyle and personality!

Our story

After the end of WW1, the young and talented Ilias Togas moves to Athens from Arcadia, a region in southern Greece. Ilias founds a small atelier at the dawn of Art Deco epoque. The high-quality craftsmanship makes Ilias Togas well-known throughout Greece.

WW2 comes to its end and Ilias Togas marries Katerina Schik, the daughter of a famous textile-manufacturing dynasty of Austrian origin. The love match leads to the union of the two businesses. Togas now combines production with retail and a unique family business empire is established.

In the early 1970s sons of Ilias, Pavlos and Dimitris Togas, inherit the family business. The brothers decide to expand the company into the home textile industry. They use the recourses of the factory and the embroidery machines while utilizing the expertise handed down to them by their father.

Since 1995, the family story grows even more with the arrival of Pavlos’ son, Kostas Togas. He is the one that will initiate the expansion of the Togas sales network to new markets all around the world. Custom service is now Togas’ priority, mastering the service and bringing it to another level.
